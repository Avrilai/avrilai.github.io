The next generation of transportation

The Hyperloop is a revolutionary, sustainable, and cost-efficient way to travel.
Each Hyperloop pod is designed to be comfortable for the passengers. Our innovative
speed control systems make acceleration and braking feel natural and gradual, while the
partially evacuated tube tracks permit speeds of over 700 mph with very little energy
expended. This means a greener, faster, and cheaper way to move.

Design

o Vacuum-evacuated tube track to drastically reduces air resistance
o Magnetically suspended pods for zero contact friction with track
o Acceleration and deceleration done entirely electrically
o Pressurized cabins to regulate environment for passengers
o Linear induction motors along track control speed of pod

Specifications

o Top speed: 760 mph
o Each pod seats 50 people
o Individual ticket price: $20

Benefits

o Considerably lower price to travel
o Much faster travel times
o Zero emissions means no greenhouse gases produced
